About the club

Garuda Games is a Gear Sports and E-Sports club—from Beyblade X and competitive spinning gear to MOBA stacks, mobile FPS like Call of Duty: Mobile, and PC play. We are built for members who want structure, hype, and real teamwork, on tables and on servers alike.

Whether you are grinding rank, chasing podium shots, or your first local win, the bird formation flies together—sharp, loud, and relentless.

Founder

Frozt started Garuda Games to grow a competitive home for gear sports and e-sports athletes who want structure, hype, and real teamwork—not casual play.

Under his lead, the club pushes pro energy in both scenes: discipline in prep, personality on stream, and respect in every match.
